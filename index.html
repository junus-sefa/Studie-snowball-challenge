About

Our mission is to create new technological solutions to end hunger. Our goal is to inspire people to take action to end hunger.

To do this we have created an app where you take the picture of your meal, and then the app calculates the average price for the meal, where you also get the opportunity to donate this amount to someone who is struggling with hunger. Where you also can track how much you have spent per month on food, and how much you donate per month.

Our goals

now - 2021

Stop hunger and secure poor people in vulnerable positions.

2021 - 2022

Make people inspired to take action and donate money

This is how it works!

First page

The first page shows how much you have donated in total. When you click the “Calculate your meal” button, the app calculates how much your meal is worth . when you click the “Donations” button, you will be able to donate the price of your meal

Calculate page

To calculate how much your meal is worth, you click on the “New picture” button to take a picture of your meal. When you click “Continue”, your meal price will be calculated!

Donate!

On the donate page the price of the meal will show up and you can choose to donate the worth of the meal to someone who needs it.
